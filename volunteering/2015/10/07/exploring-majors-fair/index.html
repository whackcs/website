    Exploring Majors Fair

    posted: 10-07-2015

    Come volunteer at the Exploring Majors Fair.

    • Where: Physics Research Building
    • Time: 11am-2pm

    Volunteers needed:

    • Shift 1: 11am-12:30
    • Shift 2: 12:30-3pm
